Hello, and welcome to my blog!
On my blog, I will be talking about my daily life, my hobbies, and some programming/coding related themes. As you may have seen on the about page, I will be posting about two to three times a week.

Themes I will cover:

  1. Programming, coding (HTML, CSS, PHP, JavaScript, C, Python...)

    I will write about what I make, and will give tips on how to do things, etc.

  2. My other hobbies (Guitar, airplanes, speedcubing, bowling, table tennis...)

    This will be about my other hobbies. I will write articles when I go to compertitions or achieve something.

  3. My daily life (School events, interesting experiences, etc.)

    This will just be about my normal daily life, whether it be about normal days or special days.

  4. Maybe some requested ideas or questions (Give me requests/questions from my contact page)

Furthermore, I will be making regular updates on this website. If you have any requests, problems, or questions on articles or the website, email me from here. Since this website is still in development, I won't post many articles for now. I am scheduling to finish the website by May 2022.

Updates to expect in the future:

  • Commenting, liking/disliking function
  • And more!
